Font Sizes:
  Font_10.wfn -  6x13 ASCII, 13x13 Big5
  Font_14.wfn -  8x16 ASCII, 16x16 Big5
  Font_16.wfn - 12x22 ASCII, 22x22 Big5

Heroine Anthem uses some incredibly non-standard font sizes. Incredibly
non-standard...

Use the corresponding dumpers/inserters to extract fonts from the wfn
files to monochrome bitmaps, then inject them back into the file.

Sample replacement fonts included in this archive are the Solaris
1024x768 console mode font (12x22) and my Windows Calligra font, adapted
from an old DOS font, rendered onto 8x16 tiles (8x16).
